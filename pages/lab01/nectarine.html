Nectarine

Description

Nectarine is a variety of peach. Peaches and nectarines are the same species even though they are sold commercially as different fruits. The species name persica refers to its original location in Persia. It was moved from there to Europe. A domesticated peach appeared in Japan in 4700-4400 BC. It appeared in India by 1700 BC and in Greece by 300 BC. Some sources claim that Alexander the Great introduced the peach to Europe.(source: Wikipedia1)
